That magical espresso-based world, filled with flavor!

Coffee is big business around the world. Every day billions of global citizens partake in a cup of steaming brewed coffee. Some people drink this liquid java in the morning, to kickstart their day. Some drink coffee in the evening as a relaxing digestif. Some drink coffee throughout the day. Some of us need decaf, to eliminate a hyper buzz, and some of us can't feel the effect of caffeine even when we want it.

Besides differing on when we drink coffee, we differ even more on how to take it. Do you like a cappucino, with a thick head of foam? Don't care for the bitter bite of espresso, how about a sweet mocha? Or do you like to take your coffee straight and dark, as a shot or more of pure espresso?

No matter how you take your coffee, I think many of us can agree that it is delicious.
